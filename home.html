WHO WE ARE

Northwest Center Against Sexual Assault (Northwest CASA) is the only full service agency that provides free services to sexual assault survivors and their loved ones within the North and Northwest suburbs of Cook County & McHenry County. Our agency has been responding to the needs of survivors of sexual violence and significant others for more than 40 years. Northwest CASA is a governing body member of the Illinois Coalition Against Sexual Assault.

OUR YEAR IN REVIEW

Responded to over 307 hospital emergency room requests for crisis service when a survivor of sexual violence sought care

Responded to over 650 hotline calls

Provided prevention education to over 30,900 students including over 10,500 high school students

Provided professional training to over 465 allied professionals

Provided over 270 survivors of sexual violence & their significant others with counseling services

Provided 24/7 telephone crisis services to over 429 survivors
